Artist Statement

Meet the Bull

The art industry has grown into a vast network of specialized and stylized individuals offering their own perspective to the world, and I’m proud to be one of them. As artists, it’s in our profession to speak volumes when no one else can, and that is why I tell stories that not only move my viewers, but I give the imagination a reality where the characters, their worlds, and their stories simply unfold before you.
